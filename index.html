About

More About Me.

Hi! I'm Laura. I'm currently completing my fourth and final year at the University of California, San Diego. A few things I love are cats, K-beauty, video games, and American politics. My friends describe me as an independent thinker. I have always excelled in my studies, and I consider my strong work ethic one of my greatest strengths. I dislike early mornings, echo chambers, and indecisiveness.

I entered UCSD originally majoring in Cognitive Science with a Specialization in Machine Learning. However, during my second year of college, I found myself immersed in an Intro to Cognitive Science course which offered a whirlwind tour of the various disciplines within the field. It was during this course that I was introduced to the particular field of study called Design and Interaction, also referred to as Human Computer Interaction.

I was drawn in due to the demand for creative problem-solving required in UI/UX Design - I am very competitive by nature, and I revel in challenges that require you to be different. While I don’t consider myself necessarily blessed with innate artistic prowess, I believe that my natural creativity and resourcefulness offer a unique perspective to every situation I encounter - a trait of far more value to the design solution process than having decent drawing skills… or at least, that’s what I like to tell myself.

Background

What I Do.

As an HCI major, my area of focus centers around the design and optimization of a system's interface. I champion the user's needs above all and implement relevant UX processes to construct intuitive, user-informed designs.

  • Usability and Information Architecture

    My understanding of the cognitive basis behind successful web and multimedia design allows me to correct the structure, navigation, layout, and user flow of a system to improve its usability.

  • Prototyping

    My experience with rapid prototyping and building interactive mockups is an essential component of the UX design process and is vital for testing user flow and generating valuable feedback.

  • User Research

    My background in usability testing, crafting personas, storyboarding, and conducting interviews/surveys are fundamental to the user-centered design process.

  • Graphic Design

    My familiarity with color theory, logo design, typography, and visual hierarchy make me a versatile UI/UX Designer with an eye for visual appeal.
